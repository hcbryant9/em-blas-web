E.M. Blasdell
Video

A central figure attempts to maintain a graceful, tranquil posture—dancing as if to please, moving under the instruction of a disembodied voice that insists on calm. Yet the feeling of serenity is disrupted by the boxing match in the background, an image of internal conflict and suppressed anger. The work explores the friction between how we are expected to feel in our bodies and how we actually do, especially in relation to gender—where performance often overrides authenticity.
